TASK: Creating and Using a Simple Keylogger:

Legal and Ethical Implications:

Legal Boundaries: Emphasize that using keyloggers without permission is illegal.
Ethical Use: Keyloggers should only be used for educational purposes in controlled environments.


Potential Defenses Against Keyloggers:

Use Anti-Malware Software: Regularly scan systems for malicious software.
Utilize Virtual Keyboards: Virtual keyboards can prevent keyloggers from capturing keystrokes.
